Research

I am an assistant professor at the Institute of Physics, Nicolaus Copernicus University (NCU), Grudziadzka 5, 87-100 Toruń, Poland.

My research involves using statistical physics and machine learning to develop advanced simulation techniques for exploring complex physical systems.

My group currently focuses on spectral map, a statistical learning method for constructing reaction coordinates to describe long-timescale dynamics.

If you are interested in working with us, drop a message at jr@fizyka.umk.pl or @jkrd.
